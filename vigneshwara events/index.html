Gowtham chriz(Independent artists)

Gowtham Chriz is a passionate and talented independent artist who specializes in creating unforgettable event experiences. With a focus on style and energy, Gowtham curates dynamic performances by other independent artists, hosts top-tier DJs, and provides delectable food and drink options. His events are designed to keep the party alive and ensure every moment is filled with joy, connection, and an electric atmosphere. As an independent artist himself, Gowtham understands the importance of supporting and showcasing the unique talents of other creatives, making his events a platform for both established and emerging artists to shine.

Tejaswi anumole(Anchor)

    Tejaswi Anumole, a captivating presence on stage, is a skilled anchor who effortlessly commands attention. With her warm smile, engaging personality, and clear diction, she guides audiences through events with grace and confidence. Whether it's a corporate gathering, a lively stage show, or an intimate gathering, Tejaswi's natural charm and ability to connect with the audience create a truly memorable experience. Her voice, a blend of warmth and authority, resonates with clarity and confidence, making her a captivating and engaging speaker. With a keen understanding of the event's flow and a knack for improvising with ease, Tejaswi ensures that every moment is seamless and enjoyable. Her passion for her craft shines through in every interaction, leaving a lasting impression on all who have the pleasure of experiencing her anchor.
